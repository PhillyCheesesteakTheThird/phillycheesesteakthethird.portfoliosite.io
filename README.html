My name is Jadan

25th May 2022 Jadan Kerr Dear Incoming Compass student, Hi! I’m Jadan, I’m a senior this year(2022-2023), and I’ve been at Compass since opening day! This place has a lot to offer, and there are a lot of opportunities for you to take advantage of while you’re here. Hopefully some of what I say will help you on your middle and high school journey. First, let’s talk about the teachers! This is arguably the greatest strength of the Compass style of learning, the relationships you build with your teachers here are unlike anything you would get at another school, you will be talking and learning from the same people year after year, they will learn your strengths, and will become genuine experts at supporting and helping you to accomplish your goals. Take advantage of this as much as possible! If there is a particular learning style, or a unique way you like to approach assignments, let them know, I’m sure they will do anything they can to support you.
